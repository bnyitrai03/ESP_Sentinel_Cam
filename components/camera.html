Camera

The camera component uses the esp32-camera driver to capture images using the Adafruit OV5640 Camera Board.

The camera pins are connected to the ESP32-S3 as follows:

The camera in QR Reader mode takes 640x480 Grayscale images. In the Camera mode it takes 2560x1600 images. The image format is read from the static config, it is either Grayscale or JPEG. With JPEG images the image size must be reduced from 2560x1600!

When the device enters deep sleep mode, the camera pins are isolated to prevent leakage current.
